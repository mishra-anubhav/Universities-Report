Columbia University, major private institution of higher education in New York, New York, U.S. It is one of the Ivy League schools. Founded in 1754 as Kingâ€™s College, it was renamed Columbia College when it reopened in 1784 after the American Revolution.

Besides Columbia College, the university includes two other undergraduate schools (the School of Engineering and Applied Science and the School of General Studies) and the affiliated Barnard and Teachers colleges.
